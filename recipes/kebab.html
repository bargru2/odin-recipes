Turkish Doner Kebab

Serve with lightly toasted pita or flatbread, chili sauce, and a salad of shredded cabbage, carrot, onion, and sliced cucumber. It's easy to make in advance, slice and freeze for whenever you get that craving!

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teasponn onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried Italian herb
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine flour, oregano, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and cayenne pepper in a large bowl. Add ground lamb and knead until thoroughly mixed together, about 3 minutes.
  3. Shape seasoned lamb mixture and place into a loaf pan; set on top of a baking sheet.
